About ePay Punjab

The first ever government payment aggregator, ePay Punjab is initiated with the vision to increase the pace of financial inclusion among the citizens of Punjab. It is a collective effort between Punjab Finance Department and Punjab Information Technology Board (PITB).

It allows to manage business to government (B2G) and person to government (P2G) payments. This system is linked to the State Bank of Pakistan (SBP) and all banks via 1-Link network.
