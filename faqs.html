Frequently Asked Questions

What is ePay Punjab?
ePay Punjab is the first ever government payment aggregator in Pakistan developed to facilitate citizens to make B2G and G2B payments digitally.
What services are currently offered by ePay Punjab?
It offers multiple tax services for various departments including Excise & Taxation, Board of Revenue, Punjab Police, and Transport Department.
How can I make payments for each levy?
You can generate a 17-digit PSID on the app and pay it via Mobile Banking, Internet Banking, ATM, or Over-the-Counter (OTC).
How can I contact the ePay Punjab team?
You can contact our support team through the official helpline number or email listed on our primary portal.
Can I access ePay Punjab if I don't have an internet connection?
No, an active internet connection is required to browse services and generate PSID tokens.
Can I use ePay Punjab if I am travelling outside Pakistan?
Yes, as long as you have your active digital banking apps or account access to settle the generated PSID code.
Can I generate PSID for payment of services which are not on my name?
Yes, you can generate a PSID for any valid asset or identification number regardless of ownership records.
What is meant by OTP?
OTP stands for One-Time Password, which is a secure code sent to your registered mobile number or email for verification.
